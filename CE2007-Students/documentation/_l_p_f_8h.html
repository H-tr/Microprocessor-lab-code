Detailed Description

implements three FIR low-pass filters

Finite length LPF
1) Size is the depth 2 to 512
2) y(n) = (sum(x(n)+x(n-1)+...+x(n-size-1))/size
3) To use a filter
a) initialize it once
b) call the filter at the sampling rate
